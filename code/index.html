Électro Rock

King Shr-P is born of a singular intention: To capture the era, the signs of the times, its light and darkness. To dig deep into the past and present to forge an image of a future that, by all accounts, promises to be bleak and brief. Hope is a double-edged sword that sustains us but also prevents us from seeing the harsh reality. King Shr-P is not a project of hope, but of stark matter-of-factness. The grooves slap you awake. The commentaries depict the world as it stands, without affectation. Can we avoid devastation, escape our sinister fate? Only time will tell. Until then, let’s enjoy the music.
